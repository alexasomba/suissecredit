About us

We provide easy access to quick loans.

We are primarily an online loan company offering loans up to N2,000,000.00 for customers who live and work in Lagos state. We commenced operations in August 2018 under a limited liability ownership structure with shareholding of N20Million, which has attracted investments of more than N500Million in the first 16 months of business. The company started with an idea to offer short term loans between N100k- N2M to customers with evidence of monthly income, a bank account from an online only portal which ensured a more confidential, accessible and convenient option to customers with busy schedules.
